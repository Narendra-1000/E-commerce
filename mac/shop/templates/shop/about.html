My Awesome Cart!

My Awesome Cart Pvt Ltd. is an Indian electronic commerce company based in Mathura, India. Founded by Narendra jadaun and Deepak singhal in 2021, the company initially focused on shoes sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon' s Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal.[5][6] as of March 2022, My Awesome Cart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.[7] My Awesome Cart is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.[8] My Awesome Cart also owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI). In August 2021, U.S.-based retail chain Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in My Awesome Cart for US$16 billion, valuing it at $22 billion.
